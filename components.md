# Flight Controller

* STM32F411 MCU
* IMU (ICM-42688-P or MPU-6050)
* Motor Driver IC x4 (one per motor)
* Voltage Regulator (3.3V)
* Radio Receiver connector
* Battery connector
* Motor connectors x4
* Crystal Oscillator
* Decoupling capacitors (as per datasheets)
* Pull-up/down resistors (as per datasheets)
